Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

CoverGuard ("we", "our", "us") operates the CoverGuard mobile application and website (coverguard.io). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from Google.

Property Search Data: We record the property addresses and locations you search for to provide our risk assessment service and improve search results.

Location Data: With your permission, we may collect your device's location to enable nearby property search. This is optional and can be disabled in your device settings.

Payment Information: Subscription payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number or banking details. Stripe's privacy policy governs payment data handling.

Usage Data: We collect anonymized analytics about how you use the app, including pages visited, features used, and performance metrics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide property risk assessments and insurance carrier information
  • Process quote requests on your behalf
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send important service updates (not marketing, unless you opt in)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sources

Our risk assessments use publicly available data from government agencies including FEMA, USGS, NOAA, Cal Fire, USFS, and the FBI Crime Data Explorer. We do not share your personal information with these agencies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Service providers: Supabase (hosting/auth), Stripe (payments), Vercel (hosting), Google Maps (visualization) — only as needed to operate the service
  • Insurance carriers: Only when you explicitly submit a quote request, and only the information necessary for the quote
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental regulation

5. Data Security

We protect your data with:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted database storage (Supabase PostgreSQL)
  • JWT-based authentication with secure session management
  • Regular security reviews and monitoring

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time through Account Settings or by emailing support@coverguard.io. Upon deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days. Anonymized analytics data may be retained.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection (e.g., location)

8. Children's Privacy

CoverGuard is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Email: support@coverguard.io
  • Website: https://coverguard.io
